Uptime is measured as a percentage of minutes in the calendar month excluding scheduled maintenance windows.

02

Scheduled Maintenance

Planned maintenance is conducted between 01:00 – 04:00 EAT on Sundays. We will post a notice in the App at least 24 hours in advance. Maintenance windows do not count against uptime calculations.

03

Exclusions

The following are not counted as downtime:

  • Scheduled maintenance windows.
  • Outages caused by your internet service provider or mobile network.
  • Force majeure events (power grid failure, floods, government-ordered shutdowns).
  • Third-party streaming CDN outages beyond our direct control.
  • CA-ordered broadcast suspension.

06

Reporting an Incident

Report outages via WhatsApp +254 715 505 284 or email info@amaicamedia.com. Include the time, your device type, and a brief description. We will acknowledge within 2 hours during business hours (Mon–Sat, 07:00–22:00 EAT).

07

Changes to this SLA

We may update targets upward (i.e. more favourable to users) at any time. Reductions in targets require 30 days' notice posted in the App.
