03

How We Use It

  • To operate the live stream, chat, polls, and leaderboard.
  • To calculate and award Pulse Points and process reward redemptions.
  • To send transactional messages (redemption confirmations, account emails).
  • To detect and prevent abuse of the rewards system.
  • To improve app performance using aggregate, anonymised analytics.

We do not use your data for targeted advertising or share it with third-party advertisers.

05

Retention

  • Account data — held while your account is active and for 12 months after deletion.
  • Chat messages — retained for 90 days, then purged.
  • Redemption records — 3 years for financial compliance.
  • Guest localStorage data — stored only on your device; cleared when you clear browser data.
06

Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Erasure — request deletion (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent — at any time for processing based on consent (e.g. airtime number).

Submit requests to info@amaicamedia.com. We respond within 21 days. You may also lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) at odpc.go.ke.

07

Security

Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access to the database is role-restricted. We notify affected users within 72 hours of a confirmed breach, as required by DPA 2019 s.43.

08

Children

We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If you believe a child has registered, contact us immediately for deletion.
