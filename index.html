These terms govern how you earn Pulse Points, redeem rewards, and claim gear. They form part of our main Terms of Service.

01

Nature of Pulse Points

Pulse Points are a non-monetary loyalty credit. They are:

  • Not currency, not a financial instrument, and not redeemable for cash.
  • Not transferable between accounts.
  • Not inheritable or assignable.
  • Subject to forfeiture if your account is suspended for abuse.

This design is consistent with the National Payment System Act (Kenya), which exempts loyalty points from e-money regulation provided they have no cash-out mechanism.

02

How Points Are Earned

Action Points Notes
Listening (per minute) 10 pts Awarded each minute the stream is active
Sending a chat message 5 pts Live community chat only
Daily streak bonus 10 × streak (max 100) Awarded on first listen of each day
Stoking the fire 1 pt Per vibe tap
Voting in a poll 10 pts One vote per poll
Setting a show reminder 5 pts Per reminder set
Referring a friend Bonus (variable) Friend must create an account

A scarcity multiplier is applied: as live listener count grows, per-action point values decrease proportionally. This keeps the points economy balanced.

03

Tiers

Tier Points Required Benefits
🥉 Bronze 0 Access to Marketplace
🥈 Silver 500 Priority support response
🥇 Gold 2,000 Exclusive poll access
🌟 Super Fan 5,000 Shoutout eligibility; gear discounts

Tier thresholds may be adjusted with 14 days' notice. Your tier is re-evaluated at the start of each calendar month based on your rolling 90-day points balance.

04

Streak Freeze

A Streak Freeze (500 pts) protects your daily streak for one missed day. Rules:

  • Only one Freeze may be held at a time.
  • A Freeze is consumed automatically on the first day you miss.
  • Freeze purchase is non-refundable once used.
  • Points deducted for a Freeze are not restored if the account is later deleted.
05

Digital Rewards (Airtime)

Airtime rewards are fulfilled by Africa's Talking to Safaricom numbers only at this time. By requesting airtime you consent to us sharing your mobile number with Africa's Talking solely for disbursement.

  • Airtime value = points redeemed ÷ 2 (e.g. 200 pts = KES 100 airtime).
  • Minimum redemption: 200 pts.
  • Maximum per day: 2,000 pts (KES 1,000 airtime).
  • Delivery is typically instant; allow up to 24 hours. If delivery fails, points are reinstated.

Airtime rewards are subject to availability and Kenya's applicable mobile money regulations. We reserve the right to suspend airtime redemption if regulatory requirements change.
